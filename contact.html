Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What is the best way to make a reservation?

For reservations, you can call us directly at +254 721 237 883, use our online reservation system on the Reservations page, or fill out the contact form above. For same-day reservations, calling is recommended.

Do you offer halal options?

Yes! All our food is 100% halal certified. We source our ingredients from trusted halal suppliers and maintain strict preparation standards. You can view our halal certification in the restaurant.

What are your parking facilities like?

We offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests. Our parking area is secure, well-lit, and can accommodate up to 50 vehicles. For large groups or events, please contact us in advance.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific requirements. Please inform us when making your reservation or speak with our staff.

What is your cancellation policy?

For regular reservations, we require 2 hours notice for cancellations. For group bookings (6+ people) and private events, please refer to our specific cancellation policies outlined in your booking confirmation.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer physical and digital gift cards in various denominations. They can be purchased at the restaurant or through our website. Gift cards are valid for one year from purchase date.
