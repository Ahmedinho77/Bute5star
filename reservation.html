Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about reservations at Bute 5 Star Restaurant.

How far in advance should I make a reservation?

We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance for weekday dinners and 3-5 days in advance for weekends. For special occasions or large groups, booking 1-2 weeks ahead is advisable.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask for at least 2 hours notice for cancellations. For large groups (8+ people), 24 hours notice is required. No-shows or last-minute cancellations may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! Our chefs are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies. Please mention any requirements in the "Special Requests" section of your reservation, and our team will ensure your dining experience is both safe and delicious.

Is there a dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. While formal attire is not required, we ask guests to avoid overly casual clothing such as athletic wear, beachwear, or flip-flops. Business casual is always appropriate.

Do you offer valet parking?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests. Our secure parking area is monitored and easily accessible from the restaurant entrance.

Can I make changes to my reservation?

Yes, you can modify your reservation by calling us at +254 721 237 883 or emailing reservations@bute5star.com. Changes are subject to availability, especially for date or time modifications.

Reservation Terms & Conditions

1. Reservation Policy

• Reservations are held for 15 minutes after the booked time. After this period, tables may be released to other guests.

• For large groups (8+ people), a credit card may be required to secure the booking.

• We accommodate special requests to the best of our ability, but they are not guaranteed.

2. Cancellation Policy

• Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before the reservation time.

• For large group cancellations, 24 hours notice is required.

• No-shows or late cancellations may be subject to a cancellation fee of KES 1,000 per person.

3. Pricing Policy

• Base price: $50 per person (includes 3-course meal)

• Group discounts: 2+ people: 5% off, 4+ people: 15% off, 6+ people: 25% off, 8+ people: 35% off

• Outdoor terrace: $10 premium per person

• Private room: $25 premium per person

• Special occasions: $15-25 premium per person based on celebration type

• Final bill may vary based on actual menu selections and additional services

4. General Information

• Our restaurant is 100% halal certified.

• We maintain a smart casual dress code.

• Complimentary valet parking is available for all guests.

• Children are welcome, and high chairs are available upon request.
