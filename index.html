Meet Our Executive Chef

Chef Alexander Moreau brings over 20 years of culinary expertise from Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe. His philosophy centers on respecting ingredients while pushing the boundaries of flavor and presentation.

Under Chef Moreau's leadership, Bute 5 Star has become renowned for its innovative tasting menus that change with the seasons, each dish telling a story of place, tradition, and culinary artistry.
